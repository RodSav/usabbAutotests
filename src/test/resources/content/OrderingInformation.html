How do I order by mail or fax?

To place your order by mail or fax, simply download and print our official order form. Fill in your shipping and billing address, item details and your method of payment (credit card, check or money order). Orders shipping to destinations in the states of Georgia, Mississippi and Nevada will be subject to sales tax. For orders that do NOT qualify for free shipping, request a Shipping/Freight Quote or contact our Freight Department at 1 (800) 448-4726 .

Ensure that all charges, including sales tax and freight or shipping fees are included. Please contact Customer Service at 1 (800) 448-4726 to verify the total, to confirm if your items are eligible for free shipping or if you're unsure of your state's sales tax rate.

Please allow 5-7 days for mail transit time, based on the distance between your location and our headquarters in GA. You will receive a confirmation e-mail from us when we receive and process your order.

Do you provide color/fabric samples?

Yes we do! We know that colors viewed online may vary slightly from the actual color due to monitor settings, or that sometimes you might need to see the fabric before you purchase. If precise colors or fabrics are important to you, please send your request for samples to info@virco.com. Include your name, your address, product item number(s) and your color/fabric selections. Samples will be sent via USPS and should arrive within 5-7 business days.

Will my item(s) require assembly?

To minimize damage during transport, many items are shipped 'Ready to Assemble'. Instructions and all the hardware necessary, with the exception of wood glue, are included.

How do I cancel my order?

We try to accommodate requests for changes or cancellations if at all possible, as your satisfaction is our priority. Please call us at 1 (800) 448-4726 or send an email to info@virco.com to cancel an order.

Cancellation requests submitted after our regular business hours or over the weekend will be processed the next business day. In rare cases, when the cancellation is received after hours, orders may have already shipped. Orders that have already shipped will be subject to our Standard Return Policy. Our representative will confirm that your order has been cancelled via telephone or email. Orders are not cancelled until confirmed by our representatives.

Orders for custom items cannot be cancelled or returned.
